Elon Musk

Business magnate and investor

Elon Musk is the founder, CEO and chief engineer of SpaceX; angel investor, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc.

Here's a time line of Elon Musk's life:

  • 1971 - Born in Pretoria, South Africa
  • 1989 -Musk arrived in Canada and lived with a second cousin in Saskatchewan for a year, working odd jobs at a farm and lumber mill.
  • 1990 -he entered Queen's University in Kingston, Ontario Two years later, he transferred to the University of Pennsylvania (UPenn), where he completed studies for a Bachelor of Arts degree in physics and a Bachelor of Science degree in economics
  • 1994 - Musk held two internships in Silicon Valley: one at the energy storage startup Pinnacle Research Institute, which investigated electrolytic ultracapacitors for energy storage, and another at the Palo Alto–based startup Rocket Science
  • 1995 - he was accepted to a PhD program in materials science at Stanford University. However, Musk decided to join the Internet boom
  • 1995 - Musk, his brother Kimbal, and Greg Kouri founded Zip2
  • 1999 - Musk co-founded X.com, an online financial services and e-mail payment company. X.com was one of the first federally insured online banks, and over 200,000 customers joined in its initial months of operation.
  • 2000 -X.com merged with online bank Confinity to avoid competition,[53][61][62] as Confinity's money-transfer service PayPal was more popular than X.com's service
  • 2003 - Tesla, Inc.—originally Tesla Motors—was incorporated by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning, who financed the company until the Series A round of funding.
  • 2022 - Musk became involved with the nonprofit Mars Society and discussed funding plans to place a growth-chamber for plants on Mars.
  • 2002 - PayPal was acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion in stock, of which Musk—the largest shareholder with 11.72% of shares—received $175.8 million.
  • 2017 -more than 15 years later, Musk purchased the X.com domain from PayPal for its sentimental value
  • 2022 -Musk discussed a goal of creating "X, the everything app
